Atlas : Sprite

Class

Atlases are good to pack textures together. Because canvas is not very optimized towards this kind of asset repackage, it won't change a lot in terms of performance for your game. However, having atlases is always good, so here you are! functionality for collision detection, however it is a bit shitty.
